- Detailed comparison of 20 PS5 games: Vanilla vs PSSR v1 vs PSSR v2
- Surprisingly SONY did not enable PSSR v2 by default when v1 is supported
- In general PSSR v2 is better (image stability, motion based effects bluring, vegetations, etc.) with equivalent performance
- Very interesting final words: "It is better to use a smaller resolution with higher quality per pixel (i.e. better and complex shading, etc.) and good upscaler rather than a higher resolution and lower quality upscaler"
